## What this replaced

Before April 2026, our Strategy page was a 53KB document with the kernel breakdown, seven-lens resource allocation, evidence-we're-wrong log, and monthly review entries. Comprehensive. And I avoided reading it weekly because it was too long.

The restructure into these six levels cut reading time from ~5 minutes to 60 seconds. All the kernel detail still exists — it lives in a linked archive page — but the surface I review every week is the one above.

The lesson: **a strategy you don't review weekly isn't a strategy. It's a document.**
