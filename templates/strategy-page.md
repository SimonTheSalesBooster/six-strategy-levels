# 🐬 [Your Business] — Strategy

> The simplest business organizer.
> Purpose → Vision → Goals → Areas → Projects → Next Actions.
> Five levels. One page. Reads in 60 seconds.

---

## 1. Purpose

> [One sentence. The thing that stays true when everything else changes. What your business is, at its core, and who it is for.]

---

## 2. Vision — End of [Year + 3]

[3–5 sentences. Paintable picture. Revenue range. Team shape. Product mix. Market position. Specific enough that someone reading it can see the business clearly.]

---

## 3. Goals — [Current Year] Annual

- [Annual revenue target]
- [Customer / user / member count]
- [Flagship distribution metric — subs, downloads, traffic]
- [One team or capability milestone]
- [One product or offer milestone]

*Rule: 3–5 bullets. Numbers only. No adjectives.*

---

## 4. Areas — Ongoing Standards

- **System 1** — one-line description
- **System 2** — one-line description
- **System 3** — one-line description
- **System 4** — one-line description
- **System 5** — one-line description

**Personal:** [Health · Finance · Family · Soul] (or your own areas)

*Rule: Areas never "finish." They're always-on standards.*

---

## 5. Projects — This Month ([Month Year])

*A Project = an outcome requiring more than one action, completed in under a year. Monthly planning slot.*

- **Project 1** — [desired outcome by end of month]
- **Project 2** — [desired outcome by end of month]
- **Project 3** — [desired outcome by end of month]
- ...

**The 5-Point Project Health Check** (run twice a week):
1. Named owner (human or agent)
2. Live next action on the dashboard
3. Method defined
4. Movement in last 7 days
5. Continuation mechanism (scheduled or ritual)

---

## 6. Next Actions

🔗 **[Link to your task system]** (Notion / Things 3 / Linear / Asana / etc.)

Suggested stages: Inbox → Clarified → In Progress → Done → Waiting For → Someday

---

## Review cadence

- **Tue + Thu, 15 minutes:** Project Health Check on each project (see section 5)
- **Weekly Friday:** full review — sweep inboxes, refresh Next Actions
- **Monthly:** refresh Projects (section 5)
- **Quarterly:** refresh Goals (section 3)
- **Annually:** refresh Vision (section 2)
- **Rarely:** refresh Purpose (section 1)
